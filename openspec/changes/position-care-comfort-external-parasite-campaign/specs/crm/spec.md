## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: B2B Prospecting Fields
The CRM MUST support professional prospecting fields needed for the Care & Comfort external-parasite campaign.

#### Scenario: Prospect record enrichment
- **WHEN** a B2B prospect is created or imported
- **THEN** the record SHALL support segment, city/province, source, source URL or source note, lead temperature, product fit, first hook, owner/upline, and next step

#### Scenario: Partner potential tracking
- **WHEN** a professional contact is evaluated
- **THEN** the CRM SHALL support tracking whether the prospect has retail shelf potential, recommendation potential, content collaboration potential, or referral-only potential

### Requirement: Campaign-Specific Pipeline Reporting
The CRM MUST allow the team to review Care & Comfort campaign progress separately from general PETS outreach.

#### Scenario: Campaign filtering
- **WHEN** the team reviews outreach results
- **THEN** the CRM SHALL allow Care & Comfort campaign contacts to be identified by campaign tag, source, segment, owner, or pipeline stage

#### Scenario: Weekly campaign metrics
- **WHEN** the team exports or reviews CRM data
- **THEN** it SHALL be possible to count contacts added, contacted, replied, interested, meetings booked, proposals sent, partners won, partners lost, and nurture contacts for the Care & Comfort campaign
