## Why

Care & Comfort can become the sharpest commercial entry point for LifePlus Pets Spain because it answers a concrete household concern: many dog owners want external-parasite protection without constant reliance on collars or topical products perceived as harsh. The recent public conversation around neonicotinoid exposure, veterinary collar precautions, children, pregnancy, and domestic contact creates a timely opening, but the campaign must stay evidence-aware, non-alarmist, and legally careful.

## What Changes

- Position LifePlus Pets Care & Comfort as the lead product for a responsible external-parasite awareness campaign in Spain.
- Create a clear claim framework that separates verified product claims, contextual education, and prohibited implications.
- Define an outreach program for veterinary clinics, pet-food shops, groomers, trainers, dog communities, and canine wellness professionals.
- Use the existing PETS Outreach CRM as the operational source of truth for prospect lists, follow-up, response tracking, and partner pipeline.
- Build a B2C and B2B content plan around "comfort without unnecessary toxic load" while avoiding disease, endocrine, or guaranteed-protection claims.
- Add a practical list-building and qualification system for centers that can be imported into the current CRM.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `care-comfort-parasite-positioning`: Defines Care & Comfort positioning, evidence boundaries, message hierarchy, and channel strategy for the external-parasite campaign.
- `professional-prospecting-program`: Defines the B2B prospecting workflow, target segments, qualification fields, CRM import rules, outreach cadence, and follow-up process.

### Modified Capabilities
- `campaign`: Adds a Care & Comfort-led campaign track that uses scientific context responsibly without converting it into medical or fear-based claims.
- `crm`: Extends CRM usage requirements for B2B prospect segmentation, list provenance, and partner pipeline tracking.

## Impact

- Affected planning: `openspec/context/`, `openspec/specs/campaign/spec.md`, `openspec/specs/crm/spec.md`.
- Affected operations: existing `crm/` app, CSV imports, contact tagging, owner assignment, follow-up process.
- Affected content: landing page, guide page, Instagram, LinkedIn, email/SMS/WhatsApp scripts, professional one-pager, FAQs.
- No immediate code dependency is introduced by this proposal; implementation may later add CRM fields, import templates, or landing sections.
