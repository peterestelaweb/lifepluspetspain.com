## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Channel Roles
Each primary channel MUST have a defined job in the campaign.

#### Scenario: Instagram role
- WHEN Instagram content is planned
- THEN it SHALL function as the main discovery, inspiration, and visual desire channel

#### Scenario: LinkedIn role
- WHEN LinkedIn content is planned
- THEN it SHALL function as the main professional credibility and outreach channel

#### Scenario: SEO role
- WHEN SEO content is planned
- THEN it SHALL function as the main demand-capture and authority channel in Spanish

#### Scenario: Professional email role
- WHEN professional veterinary outreach content is planned
- THEN email SHALL function as the structured channel for profile-specific explanation, product facts, follow-up, and careful Care & Comfort positioning
