## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Care & Comfort Lead Positioning
The campaign SHALL position LifePlus Pets Care & Comfort as the lead product for a responsible external-parasite care routine for dogs.

#### Scenario: Lead campaign product
- **WHEN** the external-parasite campaign is planned
- **THEN** Care & Comfort SHALL be the primary product and Ahiflower Oil MAY be used only as a complementary skin-and-coat support product

#### Scenario: Product role clarity
- **WHEN** Care & Comfort is described in campaign copy
- **THEN** the copy SHALL present it as a dog-only spray for external-parasite control and comfort, not as a medicine or veterinary treatment replacement

### Requirement: Evidence-Bound Messaging
The campaign SHALL separate official product facts, scientific/public-health context, and brand interpretation.

#### Scenario: Scientific study reference
- **WHEN** the 2026 neonicotinoid and precocious-puberty study is referenced
- **THEN** the content SHALL state or imply only that it is contextual evidence about prenatal neonicotinoid exposure and SHALL NOT claim that pet collars cause precocious puberty

#### Scenario: Collar precaution reference
- **WHEN** AEMPS collar precautions are referenced
- **THEN** the content SHALL use them to support practical household-contact caution and SHALL NOT claim that all collars are unsafe

### Requirement: Claim Safety
The campaign SHALL maintain a written claim map with approved, cautious, and prohibited claims.

#### Scenario: Approved product claim
- **WHEN** a content item claims parasite support
- **THEN** it SHALL stay within official product language such as helping control pulgas, garrapatas, mosquitos, moscas, acaros, piojos, and other external parasites

#### Scenario: Prohibited medical implication
- **WHEN** content is created for landing, social, CRM scripts, email, or professional materials
- **THEN** it SHALL NOT claim prevention or treatment of endocrine disruption, precocious puberty, cancer, infertility, disease, or human health harm

#### Scenario: Species limitation
- **WHEN** Care & Comfort use instructions are shown
- **THEN** they SHALL state that the product is only for dogs

### Requirement: Practical Use Education
The campaign SHALL teach the product routine clearly enough for owners and professionals to explain it.

#### Scenario: Consumer usage instructions
- **WHEN** a Care & Comfort usage asset is produced
- **THEN** it SHALL include shake-before-use, coat application, sensitive-area avoidance, cloth application for the face, reapplication every 2-3 days or after wetting, and optional bedding/tool use

#### Scenario: Professional explanation
- **WHEN** a professional-facing asset is produced
- **THEN** it SHALL include product facts, use boundaries, dog-only limitation, client explanation copy, and a recommendation to consult a veterinarian for clinical parasite-risk decisions

### Requirement: Funnel Content Plan
The campaign SHALL include awareness, consideration, conversion, and retention content for both B2C and B2B audiences.

#### Scenario: Four-week launch plan
- **WHEN** the first campaign sprint is prepared
- **THEN** it SHALL include a four-week plan covering category framing, product introduction, household routine, professional credibility, and conversion follow-up

#### Scenario: Channel adaptation
- **WHEN** the same message is adapted across channels
- **THEN** Instagram SHALL focus on discovery and routine, LinkedIn SHALL focus on professional credibility, SEO SHALL capture demand, and CRM scripts SHALL drive direct follow-up
