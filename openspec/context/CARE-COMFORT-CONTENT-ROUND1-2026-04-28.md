## B2C Post 3

### Format

Educational carousel

### Hook

`What Care & Comfort contains and how to use it correctly`

### Caption

Care & Comfort combines lemongrass oil, cinnamon oil, andiroba oil, and castor oil in a dog-only external spray.

The routine matters:

- shake before use
- spray the coat generously
- avoid eyes, nose, and mouth
- reapply as needed or after the dog gets wet

When the instructions are clear, the product becomes easier to trust and easier to use.

## FAQ Block

### Q: Is Care & Comfort a medicine?

No. It is a dog-only external spray for routine use.

### Q: Can it be used on cats?

No. The product is only for dogs.

### Q: What does it contain?

Lemongrass oil, cinnamon oil, andiroba oil, and castor oil.

### Q: How should it be used?

Shake well, spray generously on the coat, avoid eyes, nose, and mouth, and reapply as needed or after wetting.

### Q: Can it be used on bedding and grooming tools?

Yes, the official product guidance allows use on bedding and grooming tools as part of the routine.

### Q: What is the main message?

Reduce unnecessary exposure in the home while keeping your dog in a clear external-parasite routine.
