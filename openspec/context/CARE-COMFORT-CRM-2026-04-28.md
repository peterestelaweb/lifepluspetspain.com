# Care & Comfort CRM

## Purpose

Document the dedicated CRM created for the Care & Comfort campaign so it stays separate from the older PETS Outreach CRM.

## Scope

- New folder: `crm-care-comfort/`
- Audience: veterinary clinics, pet food stores, pet shops, groomers, and other dog-focused retail points
- Operational focus: Care & Comfort prospecting, follow-up, pipeline, and partner tracking

## Characteristics

- Uses its own storage key and empty data file
- Keeps only Care & Comfort-relevant import flows
- Includes Barcelona vet and store imports as the first test market
- Keeps the old PETS Outreach CRM untouched for other lines

- `crm-care-comfort/index.html`

- `crm-care-comfort/import/barcelona-tiendas-pet-50km-normalized.csv`

## Operational Note

The old `crm/` folder remains the broader PETS Outreach CRM. This new CRM should be used when the campaign is specifically Care & Comfort and the target is clinics or stores that sell dog food, pet products, or related retail services.

The neutral site version is intentionally left for a later decision. For now, only `lifepluspetspain.com` should receive Care & Comfort campaign updates.
