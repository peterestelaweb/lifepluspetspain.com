## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Care & Comfort External-Parasite Track
The campaign MUST include a dedicated Care & Comfort-led track for external-parasite awareness and responsible household routines.

#### Scenario: Campaign track creation
- **WHEN** the campaign calendar is updated
- **THEN** it SHALL include Care & Comfort content for B2C awareness, B2C conversion, B2B credibility, and B2B outreach

#### Scenario: Non-alarmist angle
- **WHEN** the campaign discusses collars, pesticides, children, pregnancy, or neonicotinoids
- **THEN** it SHALL use measured education and practical contact-reduction guidance rather than fear, blame, or disease-causality claims

### Requirement: Care & Comfort Asset Set
The campaign MUST define a minimum reusable asset set for this product track.

#### Scenario: Asset preparation
- **WHEN** the first campaign sprint is implemented
- **THEN** it SHALL produce or update at minimum a landing/guide section, one professional one-pager, one FAQ, one comparison table, one usage demo script, three social posts, and one CRM outreach script
